High-Quality, Affordable, and Compassionate Health Care

Boulder Valley Health Center provides person-centered care based on best practices in reproductive and sexual health. We strive to demonstrate compassion in everything we do. We are part of our community and dedicated to removing barriers to care, locally and beyond. Our core values are Care, Compassion, and Community.

Abortion Care

Medication and procedural abortion care up to 16 weeks, 0 days of pregnancy.

Gyn Care

Reproductive and sexual health preventive care, consultation and treatments for gynecologic concerns.

Gender Affirming

Gender affirming care, including initiation and maintenance gender-affirming hormonal therapy.

Counseling

Person-centered birth control counseling, initiation, and support.

Infection Testing and PrEP

Confidential testing, prevention and treatment for STIs including HIV, chlamydia, and gonorrhea.

Birth Control

Free and confidential birth control and STI testing for young people under age 18.

What We Do

We provide accessible and equitable reproductive and sexual health care, counseling, and information for people of all gender identities.

Who We Do It For

We provide services to everyone, including all ages, genders, sexual orientations, physical attributes, immigration, or insurance status. 60% of our patients are under age 25. We offer confidential, low or no-cost care for youth under 18, and sliding scale services for all patients.

Why We Do It

We believe that reproductive and sexual health is an essential aspect of medical care and a human right.
